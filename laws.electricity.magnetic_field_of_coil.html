Magnetic field of coil¶

Near the center of the coil, the magnetic field is quite uniform and directly proportional to the current in the coil’s wire.

Notation:

  1. \(\mu_0\) (mu_0) is vacuum_permeability.

Conditions:

  1. The medium is vacuum.

  2. The magnetic field is measured near the center of the coil.

Links:
