Speed of galaxy from distance to galaxy¶

Hubble’s Law is a cosmological law describing the expansion of the universe, according to which the recessional speed of a galaxy is proportional to the distance to it from the observer.

Notation:

  1. \(H\) (H) is hubble_constant.

Links:

  1. Wikipedia.

recessional_speed¶

Recessional speed of the galaxy, i.e. the speed at which it moves away from the observer.
