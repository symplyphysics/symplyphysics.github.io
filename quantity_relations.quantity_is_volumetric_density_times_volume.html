Quantity is volumetric density times volume¶

An extensive quantity of interest can be obtained by multiplying the corresponding volumetric density by volume.

extensive_quantity¶

Extensive quantity. See any_quantity.

Symbol:

X

Latex:

\(X\)

Dimension:

any_dimension

volumetric_density¶

Intensive volumetric density, which has the dimension of extensive_quantity divided by volume. See any_quantity.

Symbol:

rho_X

Latex:

\(\rho_{X}\)

Dimension:

any_dimension

volume¶

volume.

Symbol:

V

Latex:

\(V\)

Dimension:

volume

law¶

X = rho_X * V

Latex:
\[X = \rho_{X} V\]
