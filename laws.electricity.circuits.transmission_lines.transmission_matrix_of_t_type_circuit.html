Transmission matrix of T-type circuit¶

The T-type circuit consists of the first impedance connected in series, the third impedance connected in parallel, and the second impedance connected in series. Knowing the impedances, it is possible to calculate the parameters \(A, B, C, D\) of the transmission matrix of this line.

law¶
