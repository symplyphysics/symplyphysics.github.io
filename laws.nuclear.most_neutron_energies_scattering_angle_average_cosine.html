Average cosine of scattering angle from mass number¶

Averaged cosine of the angle of neutron scattering depends on the mass number of the target nucleus.

Links:

  1. NuclearPower.

mass_number¶

mass_number of the target nucleus.

Symbol:

A

Latex:

\(A\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

average_scattering_angle_cosine¶

Average of the cosine of the angle at which neutrons are scattered in the medium in the lab system.

Symbol:

mu

Latex:

\(\mu\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

law¶

mu = 2 / (3 * A)
