Electromagnetism¶

In general, electromagnetism is a fundamental interaction that occurs between particles with electric charge via electromagnetic fields. The studies of electromagnetic phenomena can be divided into Electrostatics, Magnetostatics, and Electrodynamics. Also see Electromagnetic waves.
