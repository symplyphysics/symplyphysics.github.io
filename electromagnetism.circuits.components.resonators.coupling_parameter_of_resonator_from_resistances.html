Coupling parameter of resonator from resistance¶

There is a coupling parameter to describe the resonator and the load. The parameter is equal to the ratio of the resonator’s resistance to the load resistance.

coupling_parameter¶

Coupling parameter of the resonator.

Symbol:

g

Latex:

\(g\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

resonator_resistance¶

electrical_resistance of the resonator.

Symbol:

R_0

Latex:

\(R_{0}\)

Dimension:

impedance

load_resistance¶

electrical_resistance of the resonator.

Symbol:

R_L

Latex:

\(R_\text{L}\)

Dimension:

impedance

law¶

g = R_0 / R_L

Latex:
\[g = \frac{R_{0}}{R_\text{L}}\]
