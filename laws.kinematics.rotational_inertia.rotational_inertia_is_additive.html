Rotational inertia is additive¶

For a system composed of several parts, its total rotational inertia is the sum of the rotational inertia of each part of the system.

Conditions:

  1. The rotational inertia is calculated for the same axis for all parts of the system.

total_rotational_inertia¶

Total rotational inertia of the system.

Symbol:

I

rotational_inertia¶

Rotational inertia of the \(k\)-th part of the system.

Symbol:

I_k

Latex:

\(I_k\)

law¶

I = Sum(I_k, k)

Latex:
\[I = \sum_k I_k\]
