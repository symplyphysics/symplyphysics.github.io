Quantity is molar quantity times amount of substance¶

An extensive quantity of interest can be obtained by multiplying the corresponding molar-specific intensive quantity by the amount of substance.

extensive_quantity¶

Extensive property.

Symbol:

X

molar_quantity¶

Intensive molar quantity.

Symbol:

X_m

Latex:

\(X_m\)

amount_of_substance¶

Amount of substance.

Symbol:

n

law¶

X = X_m * n

Latex:
\[X = X_m n\]
