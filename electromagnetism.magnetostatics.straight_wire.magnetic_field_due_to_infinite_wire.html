Magnetic field due to infinite wire¶

The magnitude of the magnetic flux density due to a thin, straight, infinite wire depends on the current through it and the radial distance to the wire.

Notation:

  1. \(\mu_0\) (mu_0) is vacuum_permeability.

Conditions:

  1. The wire is uniform, straight, and thin.

  2. The vector of the magnetic flux density is oriented in space according to the right-hand rule.
