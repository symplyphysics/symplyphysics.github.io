Admittance is conductance and susceptance¶

Admittance is generally a complex quantity whose real part is called conductance and whose imaginary part is called susceptance.

Notes:

  1. This equation can be seen as the definition of conductance and susceptance as the real and imaginary parts of admittance respectively.
