High pass Chebyshev filter from distortion and frequency¶

The approximation of the power transmission coefficient of a normalized high-pass filter is given by approximating functions of order \(n\). The Chebyshev filter is described by the function \(\left(\cos{n \arccos{f^{-1}}}\right)^{-1}\) of frequency \(f\). In this case, a high-pass filter is considered. The high-pass filter removes all frequencies from \(0\) to the set frequency.
