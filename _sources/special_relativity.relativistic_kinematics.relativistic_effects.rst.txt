Relativistic Effects (Relativistic Kinematics)
==============================================

The :ref:`Lorentz transformation` predicts some counterintuitive but correct predictions that are
time dilation and length contraction.

**Time dilation** is the difference in elapsed time as measured by two clocks because of a relative
velocity. Notably, the elapsed time is *smaller* in the moving frame as opposed to the stationary
frame.

**Length contraction** is the phenomenon that a moving object's length is measured to be shorter
than its proper (or rest) length, measured in the object's stationary frame.
