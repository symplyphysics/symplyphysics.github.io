Couplers
========

**Directional couplers** are :ref:`passive devices <Components (Circuits)>` used to couple a defined
amount of the electromagnetic power flowing in a specific direction in a :ref:`transmission line
<Transmission Lines>` to a port enabling the signal to be used in another circuit.
