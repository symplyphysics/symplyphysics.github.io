Compliance is inverse stiffness
-------------------------------

*Compliance, or flexibility*, of a spring is the inverse of its stiffness and measures
how flexible the spring is.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.definitions.compliance_is_inverse_stiffness

.. py:data:: compliance

    Compliance of the spring.

    Symbol:
        :code:`c`

.. py:data:: stiffness

    Stiffness of the spring.

    Symbol:
        :code:`k`

.. py:data:: definition

    :code:`c = 1 / k`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            c = \frac{1}{k}
