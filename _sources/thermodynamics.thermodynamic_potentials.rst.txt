Thermodynamic Potentials
========================

A **thermodynamic potential** is a scalar quantity that represents the thermodynamic state of a
system. Even though the exact meaning depends on the given thermodynamic potential, they are all
interconnected via :ref:`Legendre transformations
<Legendre Transformation (Thermodynamic Potentials)>`. *Internal energy* is an example of a
thermodynamic potential.
