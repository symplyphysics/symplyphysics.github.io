Kinetic Theory
==============

**Kinetic theory of gases** is a classical model of describing the thermodynamic behavior of gases.
Under this theory, gas is viewed as composed of a number of particles (which are atoms and molecules
comprising the gas) in random motion. The particles collide with each other and with the walls of
the container. The exact nature of the collisions depends on the model, for instance, perfect
elastic collisions are used in the ideal gas model. These assumptions are then used to solve
classical Newton's laws of motion in order to find macroscopic properties of the gas.
