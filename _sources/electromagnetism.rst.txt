Electromagnetism
================

In general, **electromagnetism** is a fundamental interaction that occurs between particles with
electric charge via electromagnetic fields. The studies of electromagnetic phenomena can be divided
into :ref:`Electrostatics`, :ref:`Magnetostatics`, and :ref:`Electrodynamics`. Also see
:ref:`Electromagnetic waves`.
