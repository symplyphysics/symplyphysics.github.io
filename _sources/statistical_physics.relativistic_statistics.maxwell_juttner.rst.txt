Maxwell—Jüttner Statistics
==========================

The Maxwell—Jüttner distribution is the distribution of speeds of particles in a hypothetical gas of
relativistic particles. In the limit of low temperatures it becomes identical to the classical
:ref:`Maxwell—Boltzmann Statistics`.
