Mechanical energy is kinetic and potential energy
-------------------------------------------------

*Mechanical energy* of the system is defined as the sum of its kinetic energy and potential energy.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.definitions.mechanical_energy_is_kinetic_and_potential_energy

.. py:data:: mechanical_energy

    Mechanical energy of the system.

    Symbol:
        :code:`E`

.. py:data:: kinetic_energy

    Kinetic energy of the system.

    Symbol:
        :code:`K`

.. py:data:: potential_energy

    Potential energy of the system.

    Symbol:
        :code:`U`

.. py:data:: definition

    :code:`E = K + U`

    Latex:
        .. math::
