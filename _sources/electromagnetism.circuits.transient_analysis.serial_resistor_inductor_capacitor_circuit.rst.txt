Serial RLC Circuit
==================

An **RLC circuit** is an :ref:`electrical circuit <Circuits>` consisting of a resistor (R), an
inductor (L), and a capacitor (C). The circuit forms a harmonic oscillator for current, and
resonates in a manner similar to an LC circuit, with the difference that the resistor introduces
:ref:`damping <Damped Oscillations>` into the system.
