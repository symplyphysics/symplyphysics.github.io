Quantity Relations
==================

Physical and chemical quantities can often be categorized as being either **intensive** or
**extensive**, according to how the property changes when the size (or extent) of the system
changes.

An **intensive** property (or quantity) is one whose magnitude is independent of the size of the
system.

An **extensive** property, on the other hand, is one whose magnitude is additive with regard to the
subsystems, i.e. they scale linearly with the size of the system.
