Serial LC Circuit
=================

An **LC circuit**, also referred to as a **resonant circuit**, is an electric circuit consisting of
an inductor (represented by the letter L), and a capacitor (represented by the letter C).

In a serial LC circuit the inductor :math:`L` and capacitor :math:`C` are connected in series:

.. image:: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b9/Series_LC_Circuit.svg/250px-Series_LC_Circuit.svg.png
    :height: 300px

An interesting property of this circuit is that it can experience resonance at the frequency

.. math::
    \omega_0 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{L C}}.
