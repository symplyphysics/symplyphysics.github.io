Energy stored in inductor via inductance and current
----------------------------------------------------

Inductors store energy in the magnetic field when the current is flowing
through it.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.laws.electricity.circuits.energy_stored_in_inductor_via_inductance_and_current

.. py:data:: energy

    Energy stored in the magnetic field.

    Symbol:
        :code:`W`

.. py:data:: inductance

    Inductance of the inductor.

    Symbol:
        :code:`L`

.. py:data:: current

    Current flowing through the inductor.

    Symbol:
        :code:`I`

.. py:data:: law

    :code:`W = 1/2 * L * I^2`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2
