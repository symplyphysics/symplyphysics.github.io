Heat of vaporization via mass
-----------------------------

*Heat of vaporization* is the heat released during the complete vaporization of a body,
in which it is converted from liquid into gaseous state.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.laws.thermodynamics.heat_of_vaporization_via_mass

.. py:data:: heat

    Heat released during vaporization.

    Symbol:
        :code:`Q`

.. py:data:: mass

    :attr:`~symplyphysics.symbols.basic.mass` of the body subjected to vaporization.

    Symbol:
        :code:`m`

    Latex:
        :math:`m`

    Dimension:
        :code:`mass`

.. py:data:: specific_heat_of_vaporization

    Heat of vaporization per unit mass of the body.

    Symbol:
        :code:`L`

.. py:data:: law

    :code:`Q = L * m`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            Q = L m
