Electric Dipole
===============

The **electric dipole moment** is a measure of the separation of positive and negative electrical
charges within a system.

A simple **electric dipole** is formed by putting two oppositely charged :ref:`point charges
<Point Charge>`. Ideally, the distance between them would be infinitesimally small, but it also
suffices if the distance from the point of observation to the dipole is much larger than the size of
the dipole itself.
