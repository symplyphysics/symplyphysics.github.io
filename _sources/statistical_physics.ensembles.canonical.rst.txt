Canonical Ensemble
==================

A **canonical ensemble** is the statistical ensemble representing the possible states of a
mechanical system in thermal equilibrium with a heat bath at a fixed temperature. The exchange of
energy between the system and the bath is allowed, and the states of the system will differ in
total energy.

it can also be referred to as the :math:`NVT` **ensemble** since the probability of states in it
depends on the temperature :math:`T`, the number :math:`N` of particles in the system, and the
system's volume :math:`V`.

For a given canonical ensemble with fixed :math:`N, V, T`, the Helmholtz free energy :math:`F`
will be constant too. Some important ensemble averages can be computed directly from :math:`F`.
