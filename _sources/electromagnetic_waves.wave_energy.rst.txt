Wave Energy (Electromagnetic Waves)
===================================

Electric and magnetic fields can store energy, and its density relates to the strength of the fields
within a given volume. In ideal (linear and non-dispersive) media, the energy density :math:`u` is
given by

.. math::
    u = \frac{1}{2} \left( \vec E \cdot \vec D + \vec H \cdot \vec B \right)

Here, :math:`\vec E` is the electric field, :math:`\vec D` is the electric displacement field,
:math:`\vec H` is the magnetizing field, and :math:`\vec B` is the magnetic field.
