Humidity
========

**Humidity** refers to the concentration of water vapor in the air. It can be measured in several
ways: **absolute humidity** is the mass of water vapor per unit air volume, and **relative
humidity** is the ratio of current absolute humidity to maximum absolute humidity at the same
temperature.
