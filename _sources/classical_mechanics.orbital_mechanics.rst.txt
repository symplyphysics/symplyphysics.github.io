Orbital Mechanics
=================

**Orbital mechanics** is a branch of celestial mechanics that studies orbiting bodies (both 
natural and man-made) in the outer space under the influence of gravity.

This module focuses on the following topics among others:

- Kepler's laws of motion
- Parameters of planetary orbits
- Potential and kinetic energy of orbiting bodies
- Escape velocities
