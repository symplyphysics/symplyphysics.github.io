Speed via angular speed and radius
----------------------------------

Speed of a rotating body can be calculated using its angular speed and instantaneous
radius of curvature of the body's path.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.laws.kinematics.speed_via_angular_speed_and_radius

.. py:data:: speed

    Linear speed.

    Symbol:
        :code:`v`

.. py:data:: angular_speed

    Angular speed.

    Symbol:
        :code:`w`

    Latex:
        :math:`\omega`

.. py:data:: radius_of_curvature

    Instantaneous radius of curvature.

    Symbol:
        :code:`r`

.. py:data:: law

    :code:`v = w * r`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            v = \omega r
