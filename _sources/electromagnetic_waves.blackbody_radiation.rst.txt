Black-body Radiation
====================

**Black-body radiation** is the thermal electromagnetic radiation emitted from a body in
thermodynamic equilibrium with its environment. A **black body** is an ideal model of a body that is
opaque (i.e. it is impermeable to light) and non-reflective (i.e. it absorbs all incoming
radiation). The radiation spectrum of a black body only depends on its *temperature*.
