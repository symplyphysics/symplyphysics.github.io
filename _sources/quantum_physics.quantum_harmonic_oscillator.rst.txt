Quantum Harmonic Oscillator
===========================

The quantum harmonic oscillator is the quantum-mechanical analog of the :ref:`classical harmonic
oscillator <Natural Oscillations>`. It is one of the few quantum-mechanical systems for which an
exact analytical solution is known.
