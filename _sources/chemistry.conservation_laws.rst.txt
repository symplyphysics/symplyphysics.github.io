Conservation Laws (Chemistry)
=============================

.. _closed-system-def:

By definition, a **closed system** is one that does not exchange any matter with its surroundings 
and is not acted upon by external forces.

A basic law of physics and chemistry holds that the total mass of a closed system is conserved,
i.e. it does not change with time.

**Notes:**

#. This is only true in the *classical* limit. In general relativity, mass is not a conserved
   quantity.
