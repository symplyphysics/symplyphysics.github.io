Hydrostatics
============

**Hydrostatics** is the branch of :ref:`fluid mechanics <Fluid Mechanics>` that studies fluids at 
rest in *hydrostatic equilibrium*, as well as *hydrostatic pressure*, which is the static pressure
exerted at a point of interest by the weight of a fluid column above the point.
