Molar mass via molecular mass
-----------------------------

The Avogadro constant is also the constant of proportionality between molar mass and
molecular mass.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.laws.chemistry.molar_mass_via_molecular_mass

.. py:data:: molar_mass

    Mass per unit amount of substance.

    Symbol:
        :code:`M`

.. py:data:: molecular_mass

    :attr:`~symplyphysics.symbols.basic.mass` of a single molecule.

    Symbol:
        :code:`m_0`

    Latex:
        :math:`m_0`

    Dimension:
        :code:`mass`

.. py:data:: law

    :code:`M = m_0 * N_A`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            M = m_0 N_\text{A}
