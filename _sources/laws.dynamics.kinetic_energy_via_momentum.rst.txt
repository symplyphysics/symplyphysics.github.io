Kinetic energy via momentum
---------------------------

Kinetic energy can be expressed as a function of momentum and mass.

**Notes:**

#. This relation also holds in Quantum Mechanics.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.laws.dynamics.kinetic_energy_via_momentum

.. py:data:: kinetic_energy

    The kinetic energy of the object.

    Symbol:
        :code:`K`

.. py:data:: momentum

    The momentum of the object.

    Symbol:
        :code:`p`

.. py:data:: mass

    The :attr:`~symplyphysics.symbols.basic.mass` of the object.

    Symbol:
        :code:`m`

    Latex:
        :math:`m`

    Dimension:
        :code:`mass`

.. py:data:: law

    :code:`K = p^2 / (2 * m)`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            K = \frac{p^2}{2 m}
