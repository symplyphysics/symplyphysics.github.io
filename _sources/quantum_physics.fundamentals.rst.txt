Fundamentals (Quantum Physics)
==============================

One of the fundamental concepts of quantum physics is the **wave function** (or **wavefunction**),
which is is a mathematical description of the quantum state of an isolated quantum system. It
provides information (in the form of probability amplitude) about what measurements of a particle's
energy, momentum, and other physical properties may yield.
