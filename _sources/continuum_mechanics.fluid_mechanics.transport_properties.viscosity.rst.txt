Viscosity
=========

**Viscosity** is a property of a fluid that quantifies the resistance force acting on fluids when 
there is relative motion between *fluid parcels*. There are different types of viscosity, namely 
**dynamic viscosity** (proportionality coefficient between shear stress and shear velocity), and 
**kinematic viscosity** (used in fluid dynamics and related to dynamic viscosity).
