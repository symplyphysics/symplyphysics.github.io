Internal energy of ideal gas via temperature
--------------------------------------------

Internal energy of an ideal gas is the sum of the kinetic energy of all of its molecules.

**Notation:**

#. :math:`R` is the molar gas constant.

**Conditions:**

#. The gas is ideal.
