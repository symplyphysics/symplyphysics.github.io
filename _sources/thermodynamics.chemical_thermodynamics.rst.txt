Chemical Thermodynamics
=======================

**Chemical thermodynamics** is the study of the relation of heat and work with chemical reactions
or physical changes of state within the domain of thermodynamics. The main goal of chemical
thermodynamics is to predict the feasibility of chemical processes (i.e. whether it will occur or
not) and the energy exchanges during chemical reactions, phase changes, and solution formation.
