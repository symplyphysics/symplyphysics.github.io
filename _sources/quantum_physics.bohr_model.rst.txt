Bohr Model
==========

The **Bohr model** is an obsolete model of the atom that incorporated some early quantum concepts.
The idea is that the atom consists of a small, dense atomic nucleus surrounded by orbiting
electrons. Among other postulates, it assumed the following:

- The atom can only absorb or radiate discrete amounts of energy, which in turn is proportional to
  the frequency of absorbed or radiated light.
- The electrons still obey ordinary mechanics in stationary orbits.
