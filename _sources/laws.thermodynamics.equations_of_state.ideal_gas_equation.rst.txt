Ideal gas equation
==================

The ideal gas law, also known as the general gas equation, is an equation of state used to
describe a hypothetical ideal gas.

**Notation:**

#. :math:`R` (:code:`R`) is :attr:`~symplyphysics.quantities.molar_gas_constant`.

**Links:**
