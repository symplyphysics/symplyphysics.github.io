Force (Dynamics)
================

**Force** is a vector quantity whose meaning can be understood (at least in classical mechanics) 
as an action that causes objects to change their velocity or their shape, or to resist other 
forces. Most common types of forces include elastic, frictional, normal, and gravitational forces.
