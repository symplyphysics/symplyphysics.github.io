Work and Heat
=============

**Work** and **heat** are both kinds of energy that appear in the description of thermodynamic
systems. *Work* is mostly understood as *mechanical work* done by forces onto or by the system's
surroundings, but it can also include other types of energy transfer, see
:ref:`Dielectric Thermodynamics`. *Heat*, on the other hand, is related to energy moving between
thermodynamic systems due to a temperature difference between them.
