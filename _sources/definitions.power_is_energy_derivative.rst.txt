Power is energy derivative
--------------------------

Power is the amount of energy transferred or converted per unit time. Equally, it is the
rate at which work is done.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.definitions.power_is_energy_derivative

.. py:data:: power

    Symbol:
        :code:`P(t)`

.. py:data:: energy

    Energy as a function of time.

    Symbol:
        :code:`E(t)`

.. py:data:: time

    Time.

    Symbol:
        :code:`t`

.. py:data:: definition

    :code:`P(t) = Derivative(E(t), t)`

    Latex:
