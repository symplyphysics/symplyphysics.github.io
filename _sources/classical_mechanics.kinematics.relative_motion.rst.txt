Relative Motion (Kinematics)
============================

**Relative motion** is motion in a reference frame that is itself moving relative to another 
relative frame. Unless the latter frame is moving uniformly, it would be a non-inertial reference 
frame. The notion of **fictitious forces** can be used to describe the motion of a body in such a 
frame.

Also see :ref:`Relative Motion (Dynamics)`.
