Inner pressure is sum of pressures
----------------------------------

Inner pressure of an ideal fluid is the sum of static, dynamic, and hydrostatic
pressures at a chosen point in space.

**Conditions:**

#. The fluid is :ref:`ideal <ideal_fluid_def>`.
