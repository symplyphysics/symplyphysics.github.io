Chemical potential of ideal gas
-------------------------------

The chemical potential of an ideal gas can be calculated from its temperature, concentration,
and thermal wavelength.

**Notation:**

#. :math:`k_\text{B}` is the Boltzmann constant.

**Conditions:**

#. The gas is ideal.
