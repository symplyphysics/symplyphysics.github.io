Diffusion (Fluid Mechanics)
===========================

In fluid mechanics, diffusion is described using **diffusion flux**, which is a vector that 
represents the quantity and direction of transfer. In the Fick's model of diffusion, it is 
proportional to the gradient of the **concentration** of the quantity in question (for example, 
the number of particles per unit volume). Thus, we can solve the equation to find the distribution 
of concentration in time and space.
