Surface charge density from charge and area
-------------------------------------------

*Surface charge density* is the amount of charge per area unit.
It is a measure of how much electric charge is distributed throughout a surface.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.definitions.surface_charge_density_from_charge_and_area

.. py:data:: surface_charge_density

    Surface charge density of the surface.

    Symbol:
        :code:`sigma`

    Latex:
        :math:`\sigma`

.. py:data:: charge

    Total charge of the surface.

    Symbol:
        :code:`q`

.. py:data:: area

    Area of the surface.

    Symbol:
        :code:`A`

.. py:data:: definition

    :code:`sigma = q / A`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            \sigma = \frac{q}{A}
