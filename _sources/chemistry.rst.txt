Chemistry
=========

**Chemistry** is the scientific study of the properties and behavior of matter related to chemical
reactions of matter with other substances. It also studies the nature of chemical bonds and
chemical compounds.

A **chemical reaction** is a transformation of some substances into one or more different
substances.
