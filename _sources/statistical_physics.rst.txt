Statistical Physics
===================

Also referred to as **statistical mechanics**, **statistical physics** is a framework that uses
probability and statistics to describe macroscopic properties of systems arising from the
collective dynamics of microscopic particles. It can be applied to both classical and quantum
systems and is inherently related to :ref:`Thermodynamics`.
