Rocket Mechanics (Special Relativity)
=====================================

**Rocket mechanics** studies the motion of vehicles propelled by the expulsion of mass (e.g.
rockets). The rocket's mass continuously changes throughout its course, thus making its motion
different from that of constant-mass bodies.
