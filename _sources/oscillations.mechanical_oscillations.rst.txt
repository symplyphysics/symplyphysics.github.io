Mechanical Oscillations
=======================

**Mechanical oscillators** are systems that perform oscillatory *motion* around an equilibrium
position. This occurs thanks to the presence of a *restoring force* that appears whenever the system
is pushed away from the equilibrium configuration and attempts to restore the equilibrium.
