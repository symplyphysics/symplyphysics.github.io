Oscillations
============

**Oscillation** is the repetitive or periodic variation, typically in time, of some measure about a
central value (often a *point of equilibrium*) or between two or more different states.

The central notions related to oscillating systems include the **period** and the **frequency**. The
period is the time needed for the oscillator to return to the same configuration. The (temporal)
frequency is the inverse of the period.
