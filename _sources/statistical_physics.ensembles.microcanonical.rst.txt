Microcanonical Ensemble
=======================

The **microcanonical ensemble** is a statistical ensemble whose total energy is fixed. Since the
energy of the ensemble cannot fluctuate, it essentially means that the system is isolated from the
outside environment.

It is also referred to as the :math:`NVE` **ensemble** because the probability of the states in it
depends on the number :math:`N` of particles, the volume :math:`V` of the system, and the total
energy :math:`E` of the system.
