Capacitors
==========

A **capacitor** is an :ref:`electronic component <Components (Circuits)>` that stores electrical
energy by accumulating electric charges on two closely spaced surfaces that are insulated from each
other. The quantity measuring the strength of a capacitor is :ref:`Capacitance`.
