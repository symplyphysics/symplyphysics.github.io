Charge is quantized
-------------------

Electric charge is quantized, i.e. restricted to certain values.

**Notation:**

#. :math:`e` is the elementary charge.

.. py:currentmodule:: symplyphysics.laws.electricity.charge_is_quantized

.. py:data:: charge

    Total charge of the body.

    Symbol:
        :code:`q`

.. py:data:: integer_factor

    Integer factor of any sign.

    Symbol:
        :code:`n`

.. py:data:: law

    :code:`q = n * e`

    Latex:
        .. math::
            q = n e
