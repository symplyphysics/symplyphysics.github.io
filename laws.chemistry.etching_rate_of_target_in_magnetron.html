Etching rate of target in magnetron¶

The ions of the gas-discharge plasma in the magnetron fall on the target and knock the atoms out of it. The sputtering coefficient shows how many target atoms are knocked out of the target by a single ion. The etching rate is how many nanometers of the target substance are etched per unit of time. In other words, this is how much thinner a target becomes per unit of time.

Notation:

  1. \(e\) (e) is elementary_charge.

  2. \(N_\text{A}\) (N_A) is avogadro_constant.
