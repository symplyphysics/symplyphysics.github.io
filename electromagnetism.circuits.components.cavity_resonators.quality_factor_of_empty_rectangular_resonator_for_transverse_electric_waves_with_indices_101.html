Quality factor of empty rectangular resonator for traverse electric waves¶

A rectangular resonator consists of metal walls and a material filling it. In the case when the resonator is empty, its quality factor depends only on the losses in the metal walls of the resonator.

Conditions:

  1. Applies to transverse electric waves with indices \(m = p = 1, n = 0\).
