Molar mass via molecular mass¶

The Avogadro constant is also the constant of proportionality between molar mass and molecular mass.

molar_mass¶

Mass per unit amount of substance.

Symbol:

M

molecular_mass¶

mass of a single molecule.

Symbol:

m_0

Latex:

\(m_0\)

Dimension:

mass

law¶

M = m_0 * N_A

Latex:
\[M = m_0 N_\text{A}\]
