Circulation is integral along curve¶

The circulation of a vector field along a closed curve is defined via a curvilinear integral of the field along the curve.

Conditions:

  1. The parametrization of the curve is a vector function of one parameter that is continuously differentiable with respect to it.

  2. The curve is closed.
