Centrifugal acceleration via centripetal acceleration¶

The vector of centrifugal acceleration has the same magnitude as the vector of centripetal acceleration but is directed oppositely to it.

centrifugal_law(centripetal_acceleration_: Vector) → Vector[source]¶

Centrifugal acceleration via centripetal acceleration.

Law

a_centrifugal = -a_centripetal

Latex

\(\vec a_\text{cf} = - \vec a_\text{cp}\)

Parameters:

centripetal_acceleration_ –

The acceleration experienced by a rotating body in an inertial frame.

Dimension: acceleration

Returns:
