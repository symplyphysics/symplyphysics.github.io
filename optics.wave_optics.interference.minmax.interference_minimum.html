Interference minimum¶

If the travel difference is equal to an odd number of half-wavelengths, then at this point of the screen there will be a minimum of intensity during interference.

Conditions:

  1. The waves must be coherent.

Links:

  1. Physics LibreTexts.

travel_difference¶

Optical travel difference between two coherent waves. See optical_distance.

Symbol:

Lambda

Latex:

\(\Lambda\)

Dimension:

length

number_minimum¶

A whole_number of interference minima.

Symbol:

N

Latex:

\(N\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

wavelength¶

wavelength of each of the both waves.

lambda

length
