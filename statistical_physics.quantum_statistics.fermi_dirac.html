Fermi—Dirak Statistics¶

Fermi—Dirac statistics is a type of quantum statistics that applies to the physics of a system in thermodynamic equilibrium consisting of many non-interacting, identical particles that obey the Pauli exclusion principle. Such particles are called fermions and they have half-integer values of spin.
