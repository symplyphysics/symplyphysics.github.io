Equilibrium voltage difference in p-n junction via concentrations¶

The p-n junction has a potential barrier preventing the movement of charge carriers.

Notation:

  1. \(k_\text{B}\) (k_B) is boltzmann_constant.

Conditions:

  1. \(n_\text{a}\) is constant on the p side and zero on the n side.

  2. \(n_\text{d}\) is constant on the n side and zero on the p side.

  3. The semiconductor is non-degenerate, i.e. \(n_\text{i}\) is independent of the Fermi energy.

Links:

  1. Size of depleting region.
