Relativistic energy—momentum relation¶

Relativistic momentum and total relativistic energy of a relativistic particle are related by a linear equation.

Conditions:

  1. Velocity \(\vec v\) and momentum \(\vec p\) must be parallel to each other.

Links:

  1. Wikipedia, derivable from here.

vector_law¶

p * c^2 = E * v

Latex:
\[{\vec p} c^{2} = E {\vec v}\]
energy_law¶

E = c^2 * norm(p) / norm(v)

Latex:
\[E = c^{2} \frac{\left \Vert {\vec p} \right \Vert}{\left \Vert {\vec v} \right \Vert}\]
