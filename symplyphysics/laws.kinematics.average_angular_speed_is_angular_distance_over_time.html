Average angular speed is angular distance over time¶

Angular speed, or angular frequency, is a scalar physical quantity measuring the rate of change in angle of rotation over time.

Average angular speed of rotation.

Latex

angular_distance¶

time¶

Time elapsed during rotation.

Symbol

law¶

avg(w) = theta / t

Latex

\[\langle \omega \rangle = \frac{\theta}{t}\]
