Period from angular frequency¶

The period of oscillations is the time it takes for the system to perform a single oscillation cycle. The Period is inversely proportional to the angular frequency of oscillations. See Angular frequency from radians per time for additional information.

period¶

Period of oscillations.

Symbol

T

circular_frequency¶

Circular, or angular, frequency of oscillations.

Symbol

w

Latex

\(\omega\)

law¶

T = 2 * pi / w

Latex

\[T = \frac{2 \pi}{\omega}\]
