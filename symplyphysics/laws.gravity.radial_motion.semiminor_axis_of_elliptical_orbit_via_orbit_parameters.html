Semiminor axis of elliptical orbit via orbit parameters¶

The minor semiaxis can be found as a function of the sector speed of the planet, the major semiaxis of its orbit, and the mass of body that attracts it, such as the Sun.

semiminor_axis¶

The semi-minor axis of the planet’s orbit.

Symbol

b

sector_speed¶

The sector speed of the planet, i.e. the area swept by the planet per unit time.

Symbol

sigma

Latex

\(\sigma\)

semimajor_axis¶

The semi-major axis of the planet’s orbit.

Symbol

a

attracting_body_mass¶

The mass of the attracting body, such as the Sun.

Symbol

M

law¶

b = 2 * sigma * sqrt(a / (G * M))

Latex

\(b = 2 \sigma \sqrt{\frac{a}{G M}}\)
