Friction force from normal force¶

The friction force is tangential interaction between two objects, which impedes there relative movement. It is proportional to the normal force between the two objects.

friction_force¶

The friction force.

Symbol

F_fr

Latex

\(F_\text{fr}\)

friction_factor¶

The friction factor between the two objects.

Symbol

mu

Latex

\(\mu\)

normal_reaction¶

The normal reaction force from one object to another.

Symbol

N

law¶

F_fr = mu * N

Latex

\[F_\text{fr} = \mu N\]
