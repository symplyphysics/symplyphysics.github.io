Kinetic energy from moment of inertia and angular velocity¶

Every rotating object bears kinetic energy, which depends on the moment of inertia of the object and its angular velocity.

kinetic_energy¶

The kinetic energy of the object.

Symbol

K

object_inertia_moment¶

The rotational inertia of the object.

Symbol

I

angular_velocity¶

The angular velocity of the object.

Symbol

w

Latex

\(\omega\)

law¶

K = I * w^2 / 2

Latex

\[K = \frac{1}{2} I \omega^2\]
