Moment of force from moment of inertia and angular acceleration¶

The moment of force (the moment of force relative to a point) is a vector physical quantity that characterizes the effect of force on a mechanical object which can cause its rotational movement.

moment_of_force¶

The moment of force applied on the object.

Symbol

tau

Latex

\(\tau\)

moment_of_inertia¶

The rotational inertia of the object.

Symbol

I

angular_acceleration¶

The angular acceleration of the object.

Symbol
