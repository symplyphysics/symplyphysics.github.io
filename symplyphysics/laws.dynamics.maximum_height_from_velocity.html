Maximum height from initial speed¶

The maximum height that a body thrown vertically will rise to depends on the initial speed.

maximum_height¶

The maximum height that the object will reach.

Symbol

h

initial_speed¶

The initial speed of the object.

Symbol

v

law¶

h = v^2 / (2 * g)

Latex

\[h = \frac{v^2}{2 g}\]
