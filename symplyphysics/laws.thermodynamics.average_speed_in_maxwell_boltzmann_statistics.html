Average speed in Maxwell—Boltzmann statistics¶

The average, or mean, speed is the expected value of the speed distribution of gas particles.

Notation:

  1. \(k_\text{B}\) is the Boltzmann constant.

Conditions:

  1. The gas is in thermal equilibrium with the environment.

  2. The gas particles are distributed according to Maxwell—Boltzmann statistics.
