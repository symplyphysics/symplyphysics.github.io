Acceleration from force¶

Newton’s second law of motion states that the acceleration of a body is directly proportional to the net force exerted on the body.

acceleration¶

The acceleration of the body.

Symbol

a

force¶

The net force exerted on the body.

Symbol

F

mass¶

The mass of the body.

Symbol

m

law¶

a = F / m

Latex

\[a = \frac{F}{m}\]
