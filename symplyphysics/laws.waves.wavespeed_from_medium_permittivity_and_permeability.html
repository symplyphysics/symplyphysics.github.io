Wave speed from medium permittivity and permeability¶

Speed of electromagnetic wave propagation depends on relative permittivity and relative permeability of the medium.

Notation:

  1. \(c\) is the speed of light.

wave_speed¶

Speed of wave propagation in medium.

Symbol

v

relative_permittivity¶

Permittivity of medium relative to that of vacuum.

Symbol

epsilon

Latex

\(\varepsilon\)

relative_permeability¶

Permeability of medium relative to that of vacuum.

Symbol

mu

Latex

\(\mu\)

law¶

v = c / sqrt(epsilon * mu)

Latex

\[v = \frac{c}{\sqrt {\varepsilon \mu}}\]
