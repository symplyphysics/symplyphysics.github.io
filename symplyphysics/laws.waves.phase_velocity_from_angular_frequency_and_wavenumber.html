Phase speed from angular frequency and wavenumber¶

Phase speed of a wave is the rate at which the wave propagates in a medium. It is the speed at which the phase of one frequency component of the wave travels.

phase_speed¶

Phase speed of the wave.

Symbol

angular_frequency¶

Angular frequency of the wave.

Symbol

Latex

angular_wavenumber¶

Angular wavenumber of the wave.

Symbol

law¶
