Tensile stress is Young’s modulus times strain¶

When an object is under tension or compression, the stress is related to the strain via the Young’s modulus.

stress¶

Stress on the object.

Symbol

sigma

Latex

\(\sigma\)

young_modulus¶

Young’s modulus of the material of the object.

Symbol

E

engineering_normal_strain¶

Engineering normal strain of the deformed body.

Symbol

e

law¶

sigma = E * e

Latex

\[\sigma = E e\]
