Centrifugal acceleration via centripetal acceleration¶

The vector of centrifugal acceleration has the same magnitude as the vector of centripetal acceleration but is directed oppositely to it.

Links:

  1. BYJU’s.

centrifugal_acceleration¶

Vector of centrifugal acceleration experienced by the body in the non-inertial, rotating frame.

Symbol:

a_cf

Latex:

\({\vec a}_\text{cf}\)

Dimension:

acceleration

centripetal_acceleration¶

Vector of centripetal acceleration experienced by the rotating body in the inertial frame.

Symbol:

a_cp

Latex:

\({\vec a}_\text{cp}\)

Dimension:

acceleration

law¶

a_cf = -a_cp

Latex:
\[{\vec a}_\text{cf} = - {\vec a}_\text{cp}\]
