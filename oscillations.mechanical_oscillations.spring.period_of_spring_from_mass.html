Period of spring from mass¶

Mass on spring is a system of object with mass \(m\) and spring with stiffness \(k\). It starts oscillating after being pushed out of balance.

Conditions:

  1. The spring does not gain or lose energy. There is no friction or inelastic deformation.

  2. The object is small enough to be considered a material point.

  3. The spring is weightless.
