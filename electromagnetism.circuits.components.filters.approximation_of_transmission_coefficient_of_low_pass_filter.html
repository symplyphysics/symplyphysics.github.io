Transmission coefficient approximation of low-pass filter¶

The approximation of the power transmission coefficient of a normalized low-pass filter is given by approximating functions of the order of \(n\).

frequency¶

temporal_frequency of the signal.

Symbol:

f

Latex:

\(f\)

Dimension:

frequency

filter_function¶

Function (of frequency) of order \(n\) which approximates the transfer coefficient.

Symbol:

F

Latex:

\(F\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

bandwidth_distortion¶

Bandwidth distortion determines the maximum distortion in the bandwidth.

Symbol:

e

Latex:

\(e\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

transfer_coefficient¶

Transfer coefficient of the filter.

Symbol:

H

Latex:

\(H\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

law¶

H = 1 / (1 + e^2 * F^2)

Latex:
\[H = \frac{1}{1 + e^{2} F^{2}}\]
