Speed of light via vacuum permittivity and permeability¶

Speed of light can be expressed using the permittivity and permeability of the vacuum.

Notation:

  1. \(c\) (c) is speed_of_light.

  2. \(\varepsilon_0\) (epsilon_0) is vacuum_permittivity.

  3. \(\mu_0\) (mu_0) is vacuum_permeability.

Links:

  1. Wikipedia.

law¶

c = 1 / sqrt(epsilon_0 * mu_0)

Latex:
\[c = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_0 \mu_0}}\]
