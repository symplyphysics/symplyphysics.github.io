Relativistic mass via rest mass and speed¶

Relativistic mass is proportional to the rest mass of the object and approaches infinity as its speed approaches the speed of light.

Notation:

  1. \(c\) (c) is speed_of_light.

Conditions:

  1. Non-zero rest mass.

Links:

  1. Wikipedia.
