Efficiency of heat engine¶

Efficiency of a heat engine is the ratio of the useful energy to the total energy received by the system.

Links:

  1. Wikipedia.

heat_from_heater¶

heat transferred by the heater to the heat engine.

Symbol:

Q_h

Latex:

\(Q_{h}\)

Dimension:

energy

heat_to_refrigerator¶

heat transferred by the heat engine to the refrigerator.

Symbol:

Q_r

Latex:

\(Q_{r}\)

Dimension:

energy

efficiency¶

thermal_efficiency of the heat engine.

Symbol:

eta

Latex:

\(\eta\)

Dimension:

dimensionless

law¶

eta = 1 - Q_r / Q_h

Latex:
\[\eta = 1 - \frac{Q_{r}}{Q_{h}}\]
