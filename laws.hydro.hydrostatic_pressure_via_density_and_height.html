Hydrostatic pressure via density and height¶

Hydrostatic pressure is the pressure exerted by a fluid at equilibrium at any point of time due to the force of gravity.

Notation:

  1. \(g\) (g) is acceleration_due_to_gravity.

Conditions:

  1. The fluid is in statical equilibrium.

  2. The only force acting on the fluid is the force of gravity.

  3. The fluid is subjected to the gravity force of Earth.
