Electric dipole moment is charge times distance¶

Electric dipole moment is a measure of the separation of positive and negative electrical charges within a system, i.e. it is a measure of the system’s overall polarity. Also see vector counterpart.

Notes:

  1. This law can be seen as the definition of electric dipole moment.

Conditions:

  1. The system can be viewed as composed of two point charges.

  2. The point charges are equal by magnitude.
